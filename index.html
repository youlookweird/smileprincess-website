About Us

Discover more about our organization!

FOUNDER

Smile Princess Foundation was formed in July 2022, by Ashleigh Chabvuta. It is an NGO that was founded as a result of period poverty that affects millions of women and girls all around the world due to menstrual myths and a lack of knowledge.

MOTIVE

The drive behind Smile Princess Foundation is to not only help those affected by Period Poverty by providing them with sanitary products but to also raise awareness about what causes period poverty.

OBJECTIVE

To raise awareness so the public and government realize how sanitary products are a need and not a want. Therefore making sanitary pads more accessible. As well as provide them to all school girls for free.
